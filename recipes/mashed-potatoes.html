Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Description

Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes are rich and creamy and so easy to make! Making mashed potatoes has never been quicker or tasted better than these Instant Pot mashed potatoes ready in 20 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 3 Pounds Potatoes
  • Water to Cover Potatoes
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Milk
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Pepper

Steps:

  1. Place peeled and sliced potatoes into bottom of instant pot
  2. Cover with water and add 1 teaspoon of salt
  3. Place lid on instant pot and set valve to seal
  4. Cook on manual pressure for 8 minutes
  5. When timer goes off, turen off instant pot and release pressure
  6. Add remaining salt, pepper, milk and butter to the potatoes. Mash umtil smooth.
  7. Enjoy!
