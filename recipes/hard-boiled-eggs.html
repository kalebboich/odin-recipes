Hard Boiled Eggs Recipe

Description

Learn how to make hard boiled eggs perfectly every time! With this easy method, they'll be easy to peel and have vibrant yellow yolks.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Bowl of Ice Water

Steps:

  1. Place eggs in pot and cover with cold water by 1 inch. Bring to boil then cover pot and turn off the heat
  2. Let eggs cook covered for 9 to 12 minutes (Depending on desired done-ness)
  3. Transfer eggs to bowl filled with ice water and chill for 14 minutes
  4. Peel eggs and enjoy!
