Penne Pasta Recipe

Description

Ready in around 30 minutes, this penne alla vodka is one of my go-to pasta dinners when Iâ€™m looking for an easy-to-make recipe that more than delivers on flavor and comfort.

Ingredients:

  • Penne Pasta
  • Butter
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Minced Garlic
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste

Steps:

  1. Put Penne Pasta in boiling water for 11 minutes or until al dente
  2. While Pasta is cooking, put sauce pan over medium heat. Then add butter. After Butter has melted, sautee garlic
  3. After sauteeing garlic, begin to add heavy whipping cream, tomato sauce and tomato paste
  4. Stir until all ingredients are thoroughly mixed in
  5. Add seasonings to desired taste then turn stove to a low simmer
  6. Add pasta to sauce when it is ready
  7. Enjoy!
